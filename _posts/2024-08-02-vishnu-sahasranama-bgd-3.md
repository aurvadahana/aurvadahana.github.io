### 18. योगः / Yogaḥ - He who (alone) is the Means [[ot]](#ot18)

**ॐ योगाय नमः**

It is stated that this Bhagavān alone is the natural and independent means for salvation. The word Yoga is derived from the verb 'yujyate' which means "(With whose help the goal) is attained". The suffix '**घञ्**' is added to form a noun by the rule - **घञ्** is added which signifies any case excepting the nominative case. [[18.1]](#f181)

The names 'Mārgaḥ' (367, 399), 'Sargaḥ' (161) and others have been derived by the addition of the suffix '**घञ्**'. The meaning of the word 'Yoga' is that He is the immediate cause of Salvation and does not stand in need of the help of anything else.

"This (Bhagavān) alone confers joy". [[18.2]](#f182)

"It is declared in the Vedas that He is the cause (for the bliss of Jīva)" [[18.3]](#f183)

"I shall set you free from all sins." [[18.4]](#f184)

## Original Text with References

<div id="ot18" style="position: absolute; left: -9999px;">;</div>

### 18. योगः / Yogaḥ [[tr]](#tr18)

asyaiva nirūpādhika-muktyupāyatvamapi vakti - yogaḥ । yujyate prāpyate anena iti । "akartari ca kārake sañjñāyām" [[18.1]](#f181) iti ghañ । evam 'mārgaḥ' (367, 399), 'sargaḥ' (161) ityādīni dhañantāni । sa sāyujyasya ananyāpekṣaḥ sākṣāt hetuḥ ityarthaḥ ।

"eṣa hyevānandayāti", [[18.2]](#f182)

"taddhetuvyapadeśācca", [[18.3]](#f183)

"ahaṃ tvā sarvapāpebhyo mokṣayiṣyāmi" [[18.4]](#f184) iti ।।

<div id="ot19" style="position: absolute; left: -9999px;">;</div>

### 19. योगविदां नेता / Yogavidāṃ Netā [[tr]](#tr19)

vyavahitopāya-niṣṭhānāmapi yogavidāṃ netā ।

nayateḥ tṛc । 'bhartā' (33), 'dhātā' (43, 951) ityādayoऽpi tṛjantāḥ । phalaparyantanirvāhakaḥ ।

"teṣāmeva anukampārtham" [[19.1]](#f191),

"teṣāmahaṃ samuddhartā" [[19.2]](#f192),

"saṃsāraṃpāramparaṃ īpsamānaiḥ ārādhanīyo hariḥ eka eva ।" [[19.3]](#f193),

"parāmṛtāt parimucyanti sarve" [[19.4]](#f194),

"juṣṭastatastena amṛtatvameti" [[19.5]](#f195), iti ।।

<div id="ot20" style="position: absolute; left: -9999px;">;</div>

### 20. प्रधानपुरुषेश्वरः / Pradhānapuruṣeśvaraḥ [[tr]](#tr20)

#### Part 1

idameva upapādyate - pradhānapuruṣeśvaraḥ । pradhānasya - bandhahetoḥ puruṣāṇāṃ ca badhyamānānāṃ sarvathā niyāmakaḥ ।

"stheśabhāsa" [[20.1]](#f201) ityādinā varac । evaṃ 'sthāvaraḥ' (428) ।

yathā -

"saṃyuktametat kṣaramakṣaraṃ ca vyaktāvyktaṃ bharate viśvamīśaḥ ।\
anīśaśvātmā badhyate bhoktṛbhāvāt jñātvā devaṃ sucyate sarvapāśaiḥ ।।" [[20.2]](#f202)

"pradhāna-kṣetrajña-patiḥ guṇeśaḥ" [[20.3]](#f203),

"kṣarātmānau īśate deva ekaḥ" [[20.4]](#f204) iti ।।

#### Part 2

evam īśvaro hi svamāyayā jīvān aparādhyato bandhanan, prapadyamānāṃśca muñcan krīḍītuṃ prabhavati ।।

"īśvaraḥ sarvabhūtānām" [[20.5]](#f205),

"daivī hyeṣā guṇamayī" [[20.6]](#f206),

"daivī devasya krīḍataḥ" [[20.7]](#f207),

"krīḍanakam" [[20.7]](#f207)

"saṃsāra-mokṣa-sthiti-bandha hetuḥ" [[20.7]](#f207),

śrīviṣṇutattve -

"tatkṛtāyāstu māyāyāḥ tatprasādaṃ vinā kvacit ।\
nāsti nirṇāśane hetuḥ saṅkṣipyetat prabhāṣitam ।।" [[20.8]](#f208) iti ।

"parābhidhyānāt tu tirohite tato hyasya bandhaviparyayo" [[20.9]](#f209),

"dehayogādvā soऽpi" [[20.10]](#f2010) iti sūtre । tācchīlya-viṣayeṇa varacā tattvatraya-bhedaḥ, īśvara-īśitavyabhāvaśca anaupādhiko jñāpyate ।

<div id="ot21" style="position: absolute; left: -9999px;">;</div>

### 21. नारसिंहवपुः / Nārasiṃhavapuḥ [[tr]](#tr21)

svabhaktyantarāya-nirvāraṇamapi pradarśayati - nārasiṃhavapuḥ । bhakta-bhayāpaha-tadapekṣā-samakṣaṇa-pratipannayathākāma-divya-mahānṛsiṃha-saṃhananaḥ ।

<div id="ot22" style="position: absolute; left: -9999px;">;</div>

### 22. श्रीमान् / Śrīmān [[tr]](#tr22)

(180, 222) ataḥ paraspara-durdhara-apūrva-vikṛtarūpa-parigrahāt api śrīmān - saundarya-lāvaṇyādibhiḥ atimanohara - divyarūpaḥ । tatra nṛsiṃhatvayoḥ sughaṭitatvaṃ paśyatāṃ śuddhimat anyat nṛmātraṃ siṃhamātraṃ vā dṛṣṭvā jugupsā jāyetāpi । tādṛgrūpa-parigraha-mātreṇaiva hi jagat trātam ।

"daṃṣṭrā-karālaṃ sura-bhīti-nāśanaṃ kṛtvā vapuḥ divya-nṛsiṃha-rūpiṇā ।
trātaṃ jagat yena .........." iti [[22.1]](#f221) ।।

<div id="ot23" style="position: absolute; left: -9999px;">;</div>

### 23. केशवः / Keśavaḥ [[tr]](#tr23)

(654) ebaṃvidha-aiśvarya-saundarya-ekānta-lakṣaṇāni-upalakṣayati-keśavaḥ ; praśasta-snigdha-nīla-kuṭila-kuntalaḥ । "keśādvoऽnyatarasyām" iti praśaṃsāyāṃ 'vaḥ' ।।

<div id="ot24" style="position: absolute; left: -9999px;">;</div>

### 24. पुरुषोत्तमः / Puruṣottamaḥ [[tr]](#tr24)

#### Part 1

sālakṣaṇya-prasaktyā paramaṃ vailakṣaṇyamāha - puruṣottamaḥ । puruṣebhyaḥ utkṛṣṭatamaḥ । 'puruṣa' śabdena cetanavācinā acetayamānā vyaktāvyaktarūpā prakṛtiḥ vyāvartyate । 'ut' iti utkṛṣṭa-vācitayā baddhāḥ ; sāmarthya-prāptena tarapā muktāḥ ; tamapā nityamuktāḥ sūrayo ananta-vainateyādayaḥ ।

idaṃ ca tebhyo vailakṣaṇyam avadya-pratibhaṭatayā vyāpana-bharaṇā-dyupakāraiḥ svāmyena ca svabhāvataḥ eva utkarṣāt ।

#### Part 2

idaṃ hi bahurbhiḥ tātparya-liṅgaiḥ paramaṃ guhyam agīyata -

"dvāvimau puruṣau loke" [[24.1]](f241) ityatra ।

"bhūmirāpoऽlano vāyuḥ" [[24.2]](f242),

"yo mām ajam anādiṃ ca" [[24.3]](f243),

"avikārāya śuddhāya", [[24.4]](#f244)

"paraḥ parāṇāṃ paramaḥ" [[24.5]](#f245)

ityādi bahu idaṃparaṃ draṣṭavyam । atrāpi bhoktṛ-bhogya-niyantṝṇām atyanta-bhedaḥ spaṣṭaḥ । ato-pariṇāma-pakṣaśca pratikṣiptaḥ । brahmaṇaḥ sarvavikārasya brahmasaṃsārasya īśvarātirikta-brahmasadbhāvasya avyakta-mukteśvarāṇāṃ brahmaṇi pralayotpādayoḥ ca abhyupagamo hi yathokta-puruṣottamatvena sarveṇa vedasiddhāntena ca virudhyate ।।
